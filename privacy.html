PRIVACY POLICY

Introduction

CreationCue watch faces are designed with your privacy in mind. We believe in transparency regarding any data related to your use of our Wear OS applications.

Data Collection & Usage

CreationCue watch faces do not collect, use, or share any personal data. Our applications function locally on your wearable device and do not require account registration or connection to external private servers for data harvesting.

Permissions

Some watch faces may request permissions to display system information (such as Body Sensors for Heart Rate or Receive Complication Data). This data is processed only to display it on your watch face and is never stored or transmitted by us.

Contact

If you have any questions or require more information, please contact us at:
